Visual cortex processes color in 13 milliseconds — before conscious awareness. Color → retina → optic nerve → visual cortex → limbic system (emotional center).   • Red activates amygdala — heart rate increase, urgency/fight-or-flight (fMRI). Why Mandate uses red.

  • Purple: 34% longer fixation times in eye-tracking. Ideal for Jonotar Kotha where deep reading matters.

Gen Z's active attention for online ads: 1.3 seconds (inStreamly, 2025/2026). They don't have short attention spans — they allocate attention based on perceived value. The hook earns that allocation. 66% of Gen Z use ad blockers. The content must EARN attention, not demand it.

inStreamly (Feb 2026) LinkedIn post: "Online ads get 1.3 seconds of active attention." Wiktoria Wójcik (Dec 2025) LinkedIn: "Gen Z loses active attention for ads in just 1.3 seconds." Center for Humane Technology.

HOOK → SUBVERSION → PAYOFF (Mandatory for Every Post)

HOOK (0-1.3 seconds): Pattern-breaker. Surprising fact, number, image, or question that violates expectations. Brain detects discrepancy → orientation response → scroll stops. Requires: specificity ("৳850 crore" not "a lot"), incongruity, human element, temporal urgency.

SUBVERSION (1.3-5 seconds): Flip expectation. Curiosity gap WIDENS. "A rickshaw puller's son — just got admitted to BUET." Brain expected poverty; got triumph. Drives further reading.

PAYOFF (5-15 seconds): The reason to share. Not information — the EMOTION or SOCIAL CURRENCY the reader gains. Insight, lesson, pride, question.
